About Me

Passionate AI Automation Engineer crafting intelligent digital solutions

Hi, I'm Yehoshua (Shui), a dedicated and passionate AI Automation Engineer with a strong drive for creating innovative AI-driven applications. My journey into tech started with a deep interest in technology and problem-solving. I've always been fascinated by how AI and automation can improve everyday life and bring ideas to life in ways that benefit people across the globe.

In July 2024, I completed a full-stack development bootcamp at Developers Institute in Tel Aviv, where I immersed myself in the world of web technologies and AI. There, I learned everything from crafting seamless user interfaces to designing robust AI systems, honing skills in Python, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and more. Building on this foundation, I've gained over 1 year of hands-on experience, specializing in agentic workflows, LLM-integrated systems, and event-driven automations using tools like AWS CDK and Playwright.

Currently, as a Junior Software Developer at eScribers LLC, I architect event-driven AI systems that process hundreds of documents daily, boosting accuracy and cutting review times. I've developed agentic Python pipelines for intelligent data extraction and orchestration, optimized systems for scalability, and translated business needs into ethical AI solutions with prompt engineering. Previously, during my frontend internship at Felix Labs, I built dynamic interfaces with React.js and TypeScript, emphasizing responsive design.

My projects, like the AI Travel Planner and my personal portfolio site, showcase my ability to integrate AI APIs for personalized recommendations while maintaining clean, scalable full-stack architecture. My approach is simple: code with purpose, automate with intelligence, and constantly seek opportunities to optimize. I'm committed to ethical AI practices and creating solutions that reduce friction, improve efficiency, and scale autonomously.
